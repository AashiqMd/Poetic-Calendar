# Week 3: My Special Year Calendar (MMV)

## 1. The Contract (I/O)

**Input:** User types in meaningful personal dates (loved ones' birthdays, milestones).

**Output:** A beautifully designed, scrollable year calendar with an AI-written warm note for each date.

## 2. The Magical Moment

Quiet, emotional reflection ("My whole year, laid out like a poem").

## 3. Scope Discipline (What we are NOT building)

Static and hardcoded only. No databases, no Google Calendar sync, no notifications, and no edit flows.

## 4. Personality / Signature Detail

Designing for the audience: is this calendar meant for quiet solo reflection, or to be printed on the family fridge?
